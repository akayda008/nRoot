Our Patrons and Partners

Our innovations by instinct enable us to serve the needs of various arms/departments/DPSU/PSUs of Government including Indian Army, Indian Air Force, Indian Navy, DRDO, BECIL and Maharashtra Police. nRoot is also in touch with various departments and agencies involved in policy making related to Atmanirbharta in Defence and Civil Sectors wherein we contribute our bit in making effective policies.

At working level, nRoot mentors MSMEs spread across the nation with the support of experienced military veterans who have first-hand experience on the most sophisticated as well as legacy equipment. Thus, nRoot is well connected to both grassroot and global level players in the industry to serve the end-users with the best products and services.
