Our Journey

As the Ambassadors of Make in India & Atma-Nirbhar Bharat campaign we synergize the efforts of enterprising technocrats, innovators, retired soldiers, professionals, agriculturists and educationists spread across the nation. Our strength is the understanding of operational needs at field level and the potential of the cutting-edge technologies. Through our innovations by instinct, we have got the distinction of being unicorns of intangibles.

One of the indigenous products, designed and developed by the associates of nRoot for the Indian Air Force, received PM's Certificate of Excellence. Mr. K Amirtha Ganesh, Director-Head R&D received the 9th CavinKare-MMA Chinnikrishnan Innovation Awards 2020 under Great Ideas Category.
Veteran Wing Commander M Jeyakumar, Chairman & Managing Director received the National Project Leader Award-2021, from Project Management Associates (PMA India).
