Our Achievements

While nRoot & its associates have been contributing relentlessly through innovative products and services, our team has also received several coveted awards and recognitions which infuse more vigour into our mission. We are privileged and honoured to inherit and receive the following awards, recognitions, and certifications.

Awards & Achievements

SIDM Champions Award-2020

Team nRoot stood Runners-up for their contribution towards Technology/Product Innovation to Address Defence Capability Gaps in the First Edition of SIDM Champions Award.

9th CavinKare-MMA Chinnikrishnan Innovation Awards 2020

Er. K Amirtha Ganesh, Director-Head R&D received the 9th CavinKare-MMA Chinnikrishnan Innovation Awards 2020 under Great Ideas Category.

National Project Leader Award-2021

Veteran Wing Commander M Jeyakumar, Chairman & Managing Director received the National Project Leader Award-2021, from Project Management Associates (PMA India).

Recognition by MoD

nRoot is privileged to have its modules (part of Projects DRISHTI & Divya DRISHTI) listed in the Joint Services Specification on Dynamic Firing Test of Ammunition (JSS 1395-01: 2021), published by MoD/GoI. It is a great feat for nRoot and its associates.

ISO 9001:2015 Certification

nRoot has established and maintains the Quality Management System as per ISO 9001:2015 standards.
